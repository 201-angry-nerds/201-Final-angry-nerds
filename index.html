OUR VISION

We believe that travel is a transformational experience, that through travel we grow in knowledge and understanding of the world around us. Travel teaches us unforgettable experiences that will be in our memories forever, let us discover other cultures, practice and learn other languages, show us different ways to express music, dance, arts. At "TAKE me AWAY" we are committed to providing our customers the best experience, from the moment that they contact us till the time that they come back home. We work on every detail prior to the trip, during the trip, and after the trip to make sure that everything will be smooth as possible for our customers. We have a vision that united we can make little changes for us but lifesaving changes for kids in need.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create the best travel experience for our customers and it starts from the moment that you contact us to the moment that you are back at home and we will do this by:

  • Matching hotels, activities, destinations with your most dreamt travel experience. We will dig deep into your past travel experiences, your likes, dislikes, your dreams, etc. so we can make sure that you will be in the best place at the right time.
  • Maintaining ourselves properly informed and trained in the always-changing travel world.
  • Looking for the best value products for our customers
  • Offering a world-class customer service experience that is open for our customers 24/7
  • Going the extra mile to obtain customer satisfaction
  • We want to help to build a better world for less fortunate children and for that reason we support organizations that work with them

ABOUT US

TAKE me AWAY is a tour operator and travel agency located in Jordan, Amman. We specialize in tours in Jordan but are always expanding and enhancing our wide range of offers, as well as adding new and exciting tours of Britain, Italy, Eastern Europe, Mexico, and the Caribbean. A TAKE me AWAY was founded in 2021 by a group of inspired students and from this humble beginning, it will grow to be a trusted resource that will help tens of thousands of people better enjoy their travel.
